**üìä Key Business Insights**
1. Operational Fulfillment Crisis

Identified a systemic operational failure in Mediterranean markets (Italy & Spain), where order completion rates dropped to ~41% versus a 90% global benchmark.

High sales volume in these regions actively destroys margin due to return costs and fulfillment breakdowns.

Impact: Demand exists, but operations convert growth into losses.

2. Customer Retention & Churn Dynamics

~80% of customers churn after Month 1, creating a severe CAC recovery problem.

36% of the customer base (10,812 users) is classified as ‚ÄúAt Risk‚Äù, representing the largest controllable revenue leakage.

Impact: Growth is front-loaded and fragile; long-term LTV is structurally constrained.

3. Marketing ROI & Efficiency

Analysis of the 2023 Global Black Friday campaign revealed a clear saturation point.

Despite increased ad spend, ROI collapsed toward breakeven, signaling diminishing marginal returns.

Impact: Scaling spend without operational and retention alignment accelerates profit erosion.

4. 2026 Revenue Forecast & Confidence

Electronics emerges as the primary growth engine with a 34.8% CAGR.

Forecast reliability is validated with low MAPE (green zone), confirming this category as a high- onfidence investment focus.

Impact: Not all growth is equal Electronics offers both scale and predictability.

Fix or Freeze

Retention Engine
